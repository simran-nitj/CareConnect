# 🛡️ CareConnect

### Protecting Seniors, Connecting Care

## 📖 About

CareConnect is an AI-powered safety and welfare platform designed for senior citizens. It helps protect elderly users from cyber fraud, provides instant emergency assistance, enables family connectivity, and supports daily well-being monitoring through an intelligent and user-friendly ecosystem.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🛡️ Detects scam calls, phishing messages, and fraud attempts
* 🚨 One-tap SOS for emergency situations
* 📍 Live location sharing with trusted contacts
* 👨‍👩‍👧 Real-time family notifications and updates
* ❤️ Daily wellness and activity check-ins
* 💊 Smart medication reminders
* 🤖 AI-powered voice assistant for guidance and support
* 🔔 Instant alerts for emergencies and suspicious activities

---

## 🎯 Problem Solved

Senior citizens are often vulnerable to:

* Cyber scams and phishing attacks
* Medical emergencies
* Delayed communication with family
* Lack of continuous welfare monitoring

CareConnect addresses these challenges through a unified platform that enhances safety, awareness, and accessibility.

---

## ⚙️ Workflow

1. Senior citizens interact with the CareConnect platform.
2. Suspicious activities are analyzed and flagged.
3. Emergency requests trigger instant SOS alerts.
4. Family members receive real-time notifications.
5. AI assistance provides support, guidance, and recommendations.
6. Welfare monitoring ensures continuous well-being tracking.
